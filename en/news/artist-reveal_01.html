The first wave of artists are revealed! A diverse group of talented individuals gathered!

We are thrilled to announce the first wave of artists! These diverse and talented artists, spanning various genres, will deliver an unforgettable experience!

U-zhaan x TAMAKI ROY x CHINZA DOPENESS

U-zhaan (tabla), Tamaki Roy (rap), and Chinza Dopeness (rap) form this unique musical unit. Their music blends the delicate and distinctive rhythms of the tabla with the humorous flows of two highly individualistic rappers, creating an unparalleled and original sound!

SAIRU

This eclectic instrumental band consists of five musicians with diverse backgrounds spanning rock, jazz, reggae, hip-hop, and world music. Their versatile and powerful sound, combined with a unique style of having an agitator continuously engaging and energizing the audience despite being an instrumental band, is guaranteed to ignite any venue!

John Nakayama Trio

This project, led by keyboardist Hideto Nakayama, explores the boundless possibilities of music, unbound by genre and utilizing jazz techniques. Their sound, a new generation of jazz incorporating elements of club music and Nakayama's exceptional technique, is guaranteed to rock any venue!

HI☆STEADY

This tropical band draws inspiration from music around the globe, transcending genre boundaries to create a sound that makes you want to dance and sway. Blending a "gumbo" of musical roots from Latin America, reggae, Africa, and blues, their vintage-infused tropical sound, emanating from a bizarre guitar, is truly one-of-a-kind!

Saya

This 19-year-old singer-songwriter began her musical journey in April 2024, guided by the concept "Music for Gloomy Nights." Her soulful voice and evocative lyrics, which offer both healing and inspiration, make her live performances a must-see!

Stay tuned for the second artist announcement! We will be updating the festival's official website and social media channels with additional information and the latest news! We look forward to welcoming you to the festival!
