ACCESS

Venue

HEARTLAND ASAGIRI

228 Nehara, Fujinomiya, Shizuoka

By Car

From Tomei Expressway
  • ➡︎➡︎About 1 hour from Fuji Interchange
  • ➡︎Take Fujinomiya Bypass after exit
  • ➡︎Continue straight for 40 min
  • ➡︎Turn right at Route 8 entrance, then left after 1km

*Follow the main road until just before arrival

From Chuo Expressway
  • ➡︎About 1 hour from Kawaguchiko IC
  • ➡︎Via Fuji Panorama Line (Route 139)
  • ➡︎Turn left at Route 8 entrance, then left after 1km

By Public Transport

  • ➡︎Take bus bound for Asagiri from Shin-Fuji Station
  • ➡︎Get off at "Route 8 Entrance" bus stop

*Access from Shin-Fuji Station is most convenient
