SAIRU

Artist Bio

This genre-bending 5-piece group delves into their musical universe through a combination of acoustic sounds and sampling. Hailing from diverse musical backgrounds, including rock, jazz, reggae, hip-hop, and global music influences, they are known for their high-energy live shows and have been a staple at festivals and events worldwide since 2006. They operate their own independent label, WONDERLUST.
