Saya

Artist Bio

With a concept that resonates with many, "Music for Gloomy Nights," 19-year-old singer-songwriter Saya began her musical journey in April 2024. Her captivating reel videos on Instagram, showcasing her delicate lyrics and melodies, have propelled her to viral fame, garnering over 100,000 followers. Saya's performances range from intimate solo sets to high-energy shows with a full band, demonstrating her versatility and dedication to her craft.
